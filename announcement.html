    MOU with PrimeForce Technologies

    June 8, 2023

    We are excited to announce a new Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with PrimeForce Technologies. This partnership represents a significant step towards the advancement of transport layer monitoring and optimization for Autonomous Networks solutions.

    PrimeForce Technologies, a recognized leader in network technology, and SelfX Now Technologies LLC, a pioneer in autonomous networks, have agreed to explore synergies and opportunities in our respective technologies. Our collaboration will focus on the intersection of PrimeForce's leading network technologies and SelfX's cutting-edge autonomous network solutions.

    This MOU marks the beginning of a journey that will leverage the strengths of both companies to deliver groundbreaking solutions in the realm of autonomous networks. By working together, we aim to accelerate the development of technologies that can transform industries, enhance productivity, and foster a new era of digital innovation.

    Stay tuned for more updates on our collaborative initiatives and joint projects. We look forward to sharing our progress with you.
